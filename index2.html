My Journey in Tech to 3MTT

Introduction

In this post, I’d like to take you on a journey through my tech career and share how I came into contact with the 3MTT programme. This initiative has been pivotal in shaping my technical skills and expanding my opportunities in the world of technology.

About Me

My name is Babangida Yohanna, a Yungur from Roma village in the Suktu district, Dumne development area, Song LGA, Adamawa State, Nigeria. I was raised in Bauchi LGA, Bauchi State, where I completed my education. As of September 2024, my highest qualification is a National Diploma (ND) in Mechatronics Engineering Technology from Federal Polytechnic Bauchi (Class of 2019).

My Tech Journey

Technology has always been a passion of mine. I first started using a computer in 2016 and began my programming journey in 2017, thanks to the Facebook AI Master Class.

I expanded my skills to web development, where I became proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Node.js, React.js, and WordPress.

How I Came Across 3MTT

Towards the end of 2023, a friend informed me about the 3MTT programme initiated by NITDA under the administration of President Bola Ahmed Tinubu. Eager to further my knowledge, I applied and was assigned to cohort 2 in May 2024.

What I’ve Gained from the 3MTT Programme

Despite not owning a smartphone and having limited access to a personal computer, my passion for learning has driven me forward. Thanks to support from my brother, Clement Johnson, and friends in the tech community, I’ve made significant progress.

Through the 3MTT programme, I gained essential skills like:

  • Prompt Engineering
  • Communication in an IT-based environment
  • Critical thinking and stress management
  • Productivity with AI
  • HTML, CSS, and OOP in JavaScript

Conclusion

If you haven’t joined the 3MTT programme yet, I strongly encourage you to do so. The knowledge gained can turn into valuable income-generating opportunities. Thanks for reading my story!
